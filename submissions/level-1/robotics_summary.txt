1) What are the key components of a robot?

Ans: A robot, in genral, has components to sense the surroundings, and itself, components to process the data from the sensors and produce a output that may be an action to perform,
 and set of actuators connected in a very designed and specific way catered for a task that actualize the action in the real world. Key components of a robot also includes
 a control mechanism that can be remotly human operated or a autonomous system, and a feedback data organisation such that proper output can be processed.
